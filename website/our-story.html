HOME MENU OUR STORY HOURS LOCATION

Located in the shadows of the Verrazano Narrow Bridge and around the block from the Fort Hamilton Army Base, Narrows Coffee Shop has been a fixture for nearly half a century.  A nostalgic feeling can be felt upon entering as three generations of family, including owners Peter and Stella Anagnostakos, their son Jimmy and his children, can all be found working the business every day.  Frequented by locals, passerby's and the military, our establishment has become a second home to many.
